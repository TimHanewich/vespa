VCOS Gallery

Thanks to the diligent work of our media team (Tim the producer, Tim Jr. and Michelle as videographers, and Chris who has recently stepped up adding his great photography skills), you can follow along on our rides from afar! We have viewers from across the globe.

Vespas & Coffee

Looking for a great first introduction to the Vespas Club of Sarasota crew? We host casual, weekly coffee meetups at O&A coffee downtown! Scoot on over!

Maintaining Your Vespa

We ride in style but aren't scared to get our hands dirty! Collectively, we have many decades of Vespa ownership and Vespa maintenance. We share tips with each other on maintaining these Italian machines!

No View Like Ours

We have several hobbyist videographers in the club! With eye-level GoPro footage and aerial views like you wouldn't believe, there's no greater way to enjoy the view!

Your Weekend Adventure

At least once a month, we hit the open road as a club! Join us for a fun ride, good conversation, and delicious food!

Vespa Parade Day

As an official chapter, The Vespa Club of Sarasota participates in the Vespa Club of America's National Parade Day! Everyone is welcome to scoot (or drive) along with us! Check out the last few years of parades!

Going the Distance

We drive near and far! We frequently rendezvous with neighboring clubs, including the Vespa Club of Fort Myers, Vespa Club of St. Pete, and Vespa Club of Tampa! Maybe we'll make it down to your neck of the woods!

Holdays are for Riding!

For special times during the year, we host holiday rides! No better way to share the festive spirit than on two wheels!
