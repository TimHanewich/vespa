Meet our Riders

Meet the faces behind the helmets! Our riders are a diverse group of individuals united by a shared passion for two wheels, adventure, and the thrill of the open road. From seasoned veterans to newbies, we're a community of enthusiasts who love to ride, explore, and experience riding with others. Get to know the riders who make the Vespa Club of Sarasota a vibrant and exciting community!

Peter

Peter, founder of the Vespa Club of Sarasota (est. 2021), enjoys riding his navy blue 2016 Vespa GTS 300! He has even rebuilt his Vespa's entire engine!

Tim

Tim is the club's unofficial videographer - he edited all the VCOS videos you see on YouTube! Tim rides a 2021 Vespa GTS Super.

Lisa

A rider of two wheels since childhood, when Lisa isn't riding her 2021 Vespa GTS Super, you can find her on a Harley-Davidson Fat Boy!

Timmy

Timmy is a Sarasota native, riding since he was only 14 years old! He rides a 2023 Vespa GTS 300 in Beige Avvolgente.

Michelle

A transplant from NY, Michelle has enjoyed soaking in the FL sun on two wheels! Primarily a passenger, you can usually spot her on the back of Timmy's GTS 300!

Mick

Since moving from Oxford, England, Mick has lived in three different climates in the US - Sarasota being his favorite! Mick rides a 2021 GTS 300 in Mint Green.

Bruce

At 87 years old, Bruce is unofficially the oldest Vespa rider in the US! Bruce is a former motorcycle rider, but today likes riding his 2010 Vespa GTS 300.

Julie

Julie, husband to Dino, rides a 2021 75th anniversary edition Primavera 150.

Dino

Dino, husband to Julie, rides a 2021 75th anniversary edition GTS 300.

Chris

The Vespa Club of Sarasota's dedicated photographer, pen maker, and puppy parent, you won't miss Chris on his yellow Vespa GTS 300!

Willy

Willy, the club's unofficial engineer, rides a white 2024 Vespa GTS Super.

Kevin

Kevin has been a hard-core two-wheeler his whole life! When he isn't riding motorcycles up the Eastern seaboard, you can find him scooting along with us!

Louisa

Louisa is one of our most experienced riders, often traveling across the country on two wheels with her husband Kevin!

Brad

Mechanically inclined, when Brad isn't working on Kat's Vespa, you can find him cruising along on his silver 2016 GTV300!

Kat

You can spot Kat on her black 2005 ET4 150, or sometimes on the back of Brad's silver Vespa GTV!

Kurt

Kurt, a seasoned two-wheel enthusiast found the yellow scooter of his dreams after a thorough search. Today, he rides in style and pride!

Madeleine

Madeleine, daughter of Lisa and Tim and sister to Timmy, is an occasional rider in the club. When she attends, you can usually see her on her mom's white GTS 300!
