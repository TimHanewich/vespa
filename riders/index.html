Meet our Riders

Meet the faces behind the helmets! Our riders are a diverse group of individuals united by a shared passion for two wheels, adventure, and the thrill of the open road. From seasoned veterans to newbies, we're a community of enthusiasts who love to ride, explore, and riding experience with others. Get to know the riders who make the Vespa Club of Sarasota a vibrant and exciting community!

Tim

Since moving to Sarasota from Attleboro, MA, Tim has owned six Vespas across two decades of ownership! Tim rides a 2021 Vespa GTS Super.

Lisa

A rider of two wheels since childhood, when Lisa isn't riding her 2021 Vespa GTS Super, you can find her on a Harley-Davidson Fat Boy!

Timmy

Timmy is a Sarasota native, riding since he was only 15 years old! He rides a 2023 Vespa GTS 300 in Beige Avvolgente.

Michelle

An implant from NY, Michelle has enjoyed soaking in the FL sun on two wheels! Primarily a passenger, you can usually spot her on the back of Timmy's GTS 300!

Mick

Since moving from Oxford, England, Mick has lived in three different climates in the US - Sarasota being his favorite! Mick rides a 2021 GTS 300 in Mint Green.

Bruce

At 87 years old, Bruce is unofficially the oldest Vespa rider in the US! Bruce is a former motorcycle rider, but today likes riding his 2010 Vespa GTS 300.

Chris

The Vespa Club of Sarasota's dedicated photographer, pen maker, and puppy parent, you won't miss Chris on his yellow Vespa GTS 300!

Brad

Brad, with his wife, Kat, represents the two-up club, always riding around with a plus one, proudly flying the flag for Vespa enthusiasts who never ride solo.

Kat

You can spot Kat on the back of Brad's silver Vespa GTV!

Peter

A naval veteran who's brought his love of flight to the road. His fighter jet-decaled Vespa GTS 300 is a tribute to his time on an aircraft carrier and a head-turner!

Kevin

Kevin has been a hard-core two-wheeler his whole life! When he isn't riding motorcycles up the Eastern seaboard, you can find him scooting along with us!

Louisa

Louisa is one of our most experienced riders, often traveling across the country on two wheels with her husband Kevin!
