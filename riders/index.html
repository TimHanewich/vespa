Meet our Riders

Meet the faces behind the helmets! Our riders are a diverse group of individuals united by a shared passion for two wheels, adventure, and the thrill of the open road. From seasoned veterans to newbies, we're a community of enthusiasts who love to ride, explore, and experience riding with others. Get to know the riders who make the Vespa Club of Sarasota a vibrant and exciting community!

Peter

Peter, founder of the Vespa Club of Sarasota (est. 2021), is currently taking a Vespa break and is enjoying being part of the Coast Guard Auxiliary as well as riding all around Sarasota on his ebike.

Tim

Tim is the club's unofficial videographer - he produces all the VCOS videos you see on YouTube! Tim rides a 2021 Vespa GTS Super.

Lisa

A rider of two wheels since childhood, when Lisa isn't riding her 2021 Vespa GTS Super, you can find her riding her Triumph Bonneville Speedmaster!

Timmy

Timmy is a Sarasota native, riding since he was only 14 years old! He rides a 2023 Vespa GTS 300 in Beige Avvolgente.

Michelle

A transplant from NY, Michelle has enjoyed soaking in the FL sun on two wheels! Primarily a passenger, you can usually spot her on the back of Timmy's GTS 300!

Mick

Since moving from Oxford, England, Mick has lived in three different climates in the US - Sarasota being his favorite! Mick rides a 2021 GTS 300 in Mint Green.

Bruce

At 87 years old, Bruce is unofficially the oldest Vespa rider in the US! Bruce is a former motorcycle rider, but today likes riding his 2018 Vespa GTS 300.

Julie

Julie, wife to Dino, rides a 2021 75th anniversary edition Primavera 150! Julie is also a talented artist.

Dino

Dino, husband to Julie, rides a 2021 75th anniversary edition GTS 300 when he isn't riding his BMW 1200 GS motorcycle!

Willy
